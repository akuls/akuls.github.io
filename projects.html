Cricket score adjustment using ML
Mar 2017 - Apr 2017

Here, we focus on prediction in a specific scenario. One where the team batting second in a limited overs game is interrupted by rain. In such a case, there is the Duckworth-Lewis method which takes into account, the overs remaining and wickets-in-hand as the two resources. Based on these, it predicts an adjusted target to be chased down once the play has resumed. However, it is evident that wickets are worth more than overs and there are other factors like venue, powerplay, momentum, etc that can affect the performance of a team. We hope to take these factors into consideration and perform a more accurate score adjustment.

Suggesting infobox templates for Wikipedia articles
Oct 2016 - Dec 2016

This project involves the suggestion of appropriate infobox templates to Wikipedia articles. Given a Wikipedia article, we try to answer two questions: Does a page require an infobox? If yes, what is the appropriate infobox to use for this page? We cast these questions as classification tasks and use linear SVMs, Decision Trees and Random Forests in our solutions. Other tools we are using include spaCy and scikit-learn.

Business Capacity Management System
Cisco Systems | Dec 2015 - Apr 2016

Designed and developed a Business Capacity Management System that automates the complete process of Capacity Management. This involved real-time tracking of customer activity using CPU, RAM, Mount-point and DB-Space usage followed by trend generation of available v/s utilized infrastructure.
